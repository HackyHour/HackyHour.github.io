About

Come to talk about Code, Tools and Science in a social environment. If you have a problem in one of those areas: bring it along and get help. We also plan to make hands-on sessions where we learn and apply new techniques or tools. You can also find companions to join a professional or fun project.

When and Where

We started this monthly meeting in Würzburg, Germany in 2015. If you have a similar meeting and want to be listed here, or if you want to start a new one at your location just open an issue.
Locations:

  • Würzburg
  • Göttingen
  • Griffith University, Brisbane
  • Cologne
  • Aberystwyth University, Wales, UK
  • Potsdam
  • Bielefeld
  • Hacky hours in Australia
  • Tucson, AZ, USA
  • DPG-Frühjahrstagungen
  • Giessen
